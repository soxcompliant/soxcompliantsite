SOX 404B vs. 404A

The transition from SOX 404B to SOX 404A involves moving from self-certification to mandatory external audits. Our team collaborates with Big Four auditing firms to ensure smooth external audits and thorough preparations.

Extracting and analyzing Segregation of Duties (SOD) matrices is a critical aspect of SOX compliance. The variations among different platforms in data extraction can be overwhelming. At Sox Compliant, we specialize in crafting ITGC (IT General Controls), ITAC (IT Application Controls), and SOD workbooks tailored to your organization's specific needs. We also develop and manage your Risk Control Matrix (RCM), identifying risks and their associated controls. From sourcing SOD data to implementing auditor-preferred controls such as 'floor to sheet, sheet to floor' checks, we equip your operations team with the knowledge and tools necessary for sustained compliance.
